Vision of Department

To outshine in edification, research and industrial services in electrical engineering in tune with societal aspirations.

Mission of Department

  1. Impart value education to produce globally competent electrical engineers proficient of extending industrial services
  2. Engage in research & progress in cutting edge and sustainable technologies.
  3. Nurture scientific personality, professional ethics and industrial cooperation.
