About newsletters

Electrical Engineering Society is the oldest student society of the Institute of Engineering and Technology Lucknow, working under the Electrical Engineering Department of the institute for enhancing the technical skills of the students. Since its inception, EES has benchmarked its efforts in providing a platform to the students of the department for exposure into the field of electrical engineering beyond the syllabus and curriculum, by arranging workshops, expert lectures and industrial visits. Maintaining its legacy of knowledge transfer from one year to another, EES bridges the gap of learning between the senior and junior students and provides a positive environment for mutual development with the help of various interaction sessions and by operating a departmental library. The core objectives and works of EES are in the direction to comply with the vision and mission of the Electrical Engineering Department and to bring out the best in the students of the department by arranging for necessary aids required for their development. If we bounce back to the history of IET Lucknow at that time when no other societies or clubs were functioning, EES organized all technical and cultural events to evolve the students to the finest. And also 'Parakram' the annual technical fest of IET was organized by this society. As due to resistors there is a voltage drop in the circuit, similarly due to some reasons flotation of participating current stopped and this society became inactive in some near past year. The day dawneth came, so was the scenario with EES and came back into action. By the efforts of students of EE department, a batch of 2016-20 the society was finally re-established on 22 Feb 2018 in the presence of Prof. Kuldeep Sahay, the honourable HOD of the Electrical Engineering Department, senior faculty members respected Prof. Bharti Dwivedi and Dr Satyendra Singh, Dr Pushkar Tripathi, Dr Arunima Verma and Faculty Adviser of EES, Dr Nitin Anand Srivastava.

Events

Visit to the Lucknow Metro Railway Corporation (LMRC) It’ll blow your mind.

On the 30th March, the Electrical department and EES conducted a one-day technical visit to the Lucknow Metro Railway Corporation (LMRC) in Uttar Pradesh for Third year students of Electrical engineering Department, IET LUCKNOW . This visit helped the students for a practical exposure in various subjects like control system, Power System.To build and run the metro, the Government of India and the Government of Uttar Pradesh formed the Lucknow Metro Rail Corporation, a 50:50 joint venture. LMRC (Lucknow Metro Rail Corporation) achieved various milestones while constructing the 8.5 km 'Priority Corridor' project between Transport Nagar and Charbagh. We would like to express our gratitude to our esteemed HOD, Dr. Seethalekshmi K Ma'am, as well as the team, for their efforts in organising the visit. Here are few glimpses of the same.

Visit to the Uttar Pradesh Power Transmission Corporation Limited (UPPCL) It’ll blow your mind.

Aone day technical visit to Uttar Pradesh Power Transmission Corporation Limited (UPPCL) Sarojini Nagar, LUCKNOW was organised for the final year students of Electrical Engineering Department, IET LUCKNOW on 31st March, 2022. UP Power Transmission Corporation Limited, incorporated under the Companies Act 1956, was incorporated in 2006 with the main objective to acquire, establish, operate, run, manage,maintain, renovate, modernize, work and use of electrical transmission lines and/or network through extra high voltage, high voltage and associated sub-stations, cables, wires, connected with transmission ancillary services, telecommunication and telemetering equipment in the State of Uttar Pradesh, India and elsewhere. The visit helped the students for a practical exposure into electricity transmission system. We would like to express our gratitude to our esteemed HOD, Dr. Seethalekshmi K Ma'am, Faculty Advisor, Dr. Nitin Anand Srivastava Sir, as well as the team, for their efforts in organising the visit. Here are few glimpses of the same.
